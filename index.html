📖 Statistics Guide

CS COMPOSITE SCORE

The Composite Score (CS) is a weighted summary of a player's key statistical contributions per game. It rewards well-rounded players who score, defend and contribute across multiple areas.

CS = (PTS × 0.25) + (STL × 0.20) + (BLK × 0.18)
      + (REB × 0.15) + (AST × 0.12) + (Grade × 0.10)
💡 Steals and blocks are weighted heavily because they directly stop the opposition. Grade contributes 10% — a player with a great letter grade but poor stats won't rank highly.
PER PLAYER EFFICIENCY RATING

PER is a single number that summarises a player's overall performance per game. It combines all major statistical categories into one score, rewarding positive contributions and penalising turnovers.

PER = PPG + (RPG × 1.2) + (APG × 1.5)
        + (SPG × 2.0) + (BPG × 2.0) − (TPG × 1.5)
💡 Assists and defensive plays (steals, blocks) are weighted higher because they are harder to achieve and have a greater impact on the game outcome.
